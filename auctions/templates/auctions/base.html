About Us

ClassLancer is a platform designed to allow students to seamlessly become teachers or students based on their current needs. Students can create classes corresponding to their academic strengths that can help other students gain a better understanding of those very subjects, while also being able to sign up for classes that focus on their weaker subjects. Thus, this makes ClassLancer a dynamic platform that focuses on providing convenience to users who wish to enhance their learning by either choosing to teach or learn. All of our tutors go through a verification process, and are certified before they are allowed to create classes. So what are you waiting for? Embark on your learning journey with us!

Our Story

Sometimes, circumstances lead you to meet people you would have never expected to meet. But life happens. Aditya and Ee Jin were first introduced to each other while completing their Basic Military Training (BMT) at the start of their 22 months of National Service. Over the course of 2 months on Tekong Island, they not only became closest friends, but also discovered that they had a shared passion for creating something using technology that would positively impact the lives of others. Aditya and Ee Jin kept in touch even after their BMT, constantly discussing proposals with dreams of making it big. During this time, Aditya bumped into Yash, with whom he spent only about 15 minutes before discovering that Yash too shared a similar passion. They also realised that they would be attending the same course at the same university in 2 years time. Inevitably, they started to get closer and closer, until one day Aditya recognized the opportunity in introducing Ee Jin and Yash to each other. The trio debated possible startup ideas for months, rejecting over 30 suggestions until eventually agreeing upon the concept of ClassLancer, which today they proudly call a company of their own.
